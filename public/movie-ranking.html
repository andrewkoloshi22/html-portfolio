Andrew's Top 3 Movies of All Time

These are the best movies according to Andrew

The Dark Knight

Not just a super-hero movie but a crime drama about a group of people who step up to clean their city. Three people who work together as a symbol of hope. Nobody has ever played a better villain than Heath Ledger in this film.

Django Unchained

A film about the troubles of slavery with a somewhat lightheardted tone. Christoph Waltz's performance as the slave-freeing bounty hunter is mezmorizing and is a real bright spot on this look into a sour history.

Planes, Trains & Automobiles

Thanksgiving being my favorite holiday, it's only fitting this would be in here. As a lover of all things John Candy and any buddy road trip movies, this movie is has everything i could ever want. Great laughs, great heart and two great comedians who play off of eachother perfectly.
